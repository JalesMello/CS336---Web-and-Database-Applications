Welcome - Business Conference 2017

Hello and welcome to this business conference. This conference is made up of 3 sessions and 3 workshops per session. The 1st session is called Sales and Marketing and consists of workshops on topics regarding Client Procedures, Contracts, and Closing Techniques. The 2nd session is called Accounting and H.R. and consists of workshops on topics regarding Accounting, H.R., and Legal/Tax matters. Lastly, the 3rd session consists of workshops regarding Production, Purchasing, and Project Coordination.

The business conference starts on July 1st 2017 at 09:00 PST.
